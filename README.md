## Overview of Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector

Citizen Sleeper 2: Starward Vector is an immersive role-playing game set in a captivating sci-fi universe. Players step into the shoes of a sentient android, struggling to survive after escaping from an oppressive corporation. With a fractured body and a target on your back, the game combines deep narrative exploration with strategic choices. It utilizes a unique dice-based mechanic that drives the story forward, offering players a chance to craft their own path.

In this game, your decisions shape your experience, from managing your physical condition to deciding who you trust in a galaxy full of danger. The sequel expands on the original’s formula, offering new characters, environments, and challenges. Whether you're engaged in intense combat or navigating delicate social situations, Citizen Sleeper 2 provides a complex and rewarding experience for every player.

This game is a perfect blend of strategy, survival, and storytelling. It is set to launch on multiple platforms, including PC, Nintendo Switch, and PlayStation, ensuring fans across the globe can dive into the world of Citizen Sleeper.
